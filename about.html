About Me

The reason i do this.

As I lie on the couch, holding a book in my hands or immersing myself in a TV show through the screen, it feels like I enter another world. The tranquil yet fulfilling sensation is akin to traversing time and space, experiencing adventures and emotional twists alongside the authors or actors.

For me, reading is not just a pastime; it is a journey of the soul. When engrossed in a novel's plot, it feels like being part of it, sensing the joys and sorrows of each character. The allure of books lies in their ability to inspire contemplation, evoke resonance from the depths of the heart, and provide me with a wealth of knowledge and emotional experiences.

On the other hand, films and TV shows offer a visual feast. Through the storytelling and the actors' performances on the screen, I can immerse myself in different narrative worlds. Sometimes, a movie or TV series goes beyond mere entertainment, delving into social phenomena and exploring the depths of human nature, offering both enjoyment and opportunities for reflection.

Sharing these viewing experiences is a way for me to engage with others. Discussing with friends or online acquaintances, I gain diverse perspectives and insights, enriching my reading and viewing experiences. Simultaneously, I discover a community of like-minded individuals who, like me, are on a quest for literature and cinematic wonders, sharing thoughts and recommendations.

In summary, reading and watching have become an integral part of my life. They not only satisfy my entertainment needs but also enrich my inner world. Through words and visuals, I've established a subtle yet beautiful connection with authors, actors, and fellow viewers. This sense of empathy and resonance makes me cherish and love each opportunity for reading and viewing even more.
